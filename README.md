# BlueArika

## Premise

You are **Asa Webb**, a recently transferred student arriving in **Kasato** — a quiet, isolated town in Japan hiding a dangerous secret beneath its calm surface.

Shortly after settling in, you encounter a strange cat that seems to know far more about the town than it should. It warns you about a series of unexplained occurrences connected to a growing mystery known only as **the Blue Arika**.

Now, alongside a group of newfound friends, you must uncover the truth behind Kasato’s secrets before the town is consumed by them.
